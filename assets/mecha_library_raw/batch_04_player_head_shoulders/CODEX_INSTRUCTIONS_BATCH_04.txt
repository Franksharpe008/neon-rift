Titan Blocks Premium Mecha Assets, Batch 04
Player head, helmet, visor, shoulders, neck connector, and shoulder thruster assets

Purpose:
This batch contains 10 individual top-down mecha assets for the main player identity layer. These files should be used for head, visor, shoulder plates, neck connector, core light, and shoulder thruster visuals.

Camera rule:
Every asset in this batch is intended to be 100% top-down / direct overhead, matching the V4 pack camera logic. Do not treat these as side-view or isometric art.

Background rule:
These files use PNG-style checkerboard/transparent presentation from generation. In final game integration, treat checkerboard pixels as alpha/background cleanup if needed. Do not preserve checkerboard backgrounds in the final game render.

Gameplay rule:
Do not change gameplay mechanics. Preserve movement, hitboxes, arm motion, leg motion, gun rotation, recoil, dash, power-ups, scoring, music, HUD, enemy AI, and boss logic.

Integration rule:
Do not paste these as one full character sheet. These are individual assets. Sort by function into the final player wrap folder.

Recommended final folders:
assets/player_wrap_v6/head_wraps/
assets/player_wrap_v6/shoulder_wraps/
assets/player_wrap_v6/thruster_frames/
